1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer or mobile device) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and content based on your interests.
