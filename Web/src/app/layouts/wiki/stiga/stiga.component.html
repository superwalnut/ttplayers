Stiga

Stiga is a Swedish brand that is known for its high-quality table tennis equipment. It was founded in 1944 and has been producing blades, rubbers, balls, tables, shoes, and clothing for table tennis players of all levels. Stiga sponsors many top players and international tournaments, such as the World Table Tennis Championships and the World Cup. Stiga products are designed to combine innovation, technology, and style.
