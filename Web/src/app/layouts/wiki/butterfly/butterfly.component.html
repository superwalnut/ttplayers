Butterfly

Butterfly is a prominent Japanese brand specializing in high-quality table tennis equipment. Renowned for its innovation and craftsmanship, Butterfly has been a leader in the table tennis industry for decades. They are known for producing top-tier equipment including blades, rubbers, balls, tables, and accessories used by professional and amateur players worldwide. Their products are crafted with a focus on cutting-edge technology, performance, and precision, catering to a wide range of player styles and skill levels. Butterfly's commitment to excellence has solidified its position as one of the most trusted and sought-after brands in the realm of table tennis.

Butterfly Rubbers

Butterfly rubbers are available in black and red top sheet colors for each rubber. The top sheet is the surface on which the ball hits, and the sponge is the layer under the top sheet that affects the speed, spin, and control of the rubber.
