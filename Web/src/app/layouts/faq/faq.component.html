 What is ttPlayers' data source?
All table tennis players' data is from rating central's Australian events, matches and players. They are all public data that is accessible to everyone online.
Why my rating is not updated?
Players' rating are usually updated after a few days of the match. We do regularly updating players' ratings, rankings and all statistics data.

How to Reduce the standard deviation?
The simple answer is to play more matches and you will get a relatively accurate rating, and your rating's standard deviation will reduce too.
What should I do if my match history is wrong?
If you think one or more of your matches are having incorrect scores, please send us an email with your player link, event name and date, match details and your opponent details. We will fix it asap.
